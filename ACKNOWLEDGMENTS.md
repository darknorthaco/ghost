# Acknowledgments

## Retrieval–weight experiment (sibling project)

GHOST’s **hybrid retrieval** and **adaptive weighting** stack (BM25 + dense embeddings + RRF, Thompson Sampling bandit, explainable scoring) builds on concepts, experiments, and code lineage from the **retrieval-weight experiment** — developed by **[Alfonso DiRocco](https://github.com/fonz-ai)** ([@fonz-ai](https://github.com/fonz-ai)), the author’s brother.

We are grateful for that foundation. GHOST is a **new integrated system** (it is not a copy of that repo end-to-end); the acknowledgment here is for **intellectual and engineering lineage** in the retrieval layer.
